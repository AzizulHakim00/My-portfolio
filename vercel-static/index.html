MICROWAVE · METAMATERIAL · ML · ABSTRACT

Dual-Band Sensor

This work presents a dual-band single-negative metamaterial sensor for microwave soil-moisture sensing using CST full-wave simulation. The unit cell combines a modified rectangular resonator frame, four corner spiral resonators, and a central split circular resonator loaded by a vertical strip line. Sandy and loamy soils are studied in a Floquet-port sandwich configuration. Two moisture-dependent resonance bands appear at 2.480 GHz and 5.055 GHz, with linear calibration sensitivities of 11.82 and 14.92 MHz per percentage point for sandy soil and 16.94 and 19.60 MHz per percentage point for loamy soil. All fitted calibration models achieve R² above 0.98. Field maps and an equivalent LC topology explain the frequency shifts, while a preliminary machine-learning analysis demonstrates automated interpretation of the extracted resonance features.

RELIABLE CLINICAL AI · ABSTRACT

Thyro-RIFT

Thyro-RIFT is a missingness-aware and reliability-informed feature transformer for structured thyroid-disease prediction using clinical and laboratory records. It combines missingness masks, uncertainty indicators, clinical-group embeddings, reliability-guided transformer encoding, corrupted-input training, multi-seed prediction, validation-selected thresholds and probability calibration. The framework is evaluated across six thyroid benchmark endpoints using official held-out tests, cross-validation, baseline comparison, ablation analysis, robustness testing and retrospective external/domain validation.
